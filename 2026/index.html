Third Israeli Privacy and Fairness Workshop

Date: Monday, July 20th, 2026

Location: The Alexander Grass Computer Science Building, Bar-Ilan University

Overview

This one day workshop is intended to bring together researchers and students from our community (in the broad sense of the word), and will include a combination of full length and short talks by some of the participants. We hope to include a poster session this year.

The target audience includes academic and academic-adjacent individuals in Israel working in the fields of Privacy and Fairness.
